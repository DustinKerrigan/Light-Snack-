Bio
Introducing Dylan Cook, the tenacious college athlete hailing from Tucson, Arizona, whose journey from Ironwood Ridge High School to Morehead State is a testament to his unyielding commitment to excellence. Named first team all Arizona during his high school years, Dylan’s football prowess was evident through his stats: 130+ tackles, 16 interceptions, and 12 touchdowns in just 3 seasons. Despite setbacks of a canceled senior season due to COVID-19, Dylan’s dedication shined as he made his mark at Morehead State University, appearing in all 11 games as a redshirt freshman and now gearing up to start at safety for the Eagles. Beyond his football achievements, Dylan boasts a collection of over 100 sneakers, and has achieved world ranking as a Madden player.
